### Coordinate Systems
```

    |____X       Y____|
   /               /
  /               /
 Y               X

```

- **Right-hand rule**: Curl fingers from x to y, thumb points to z
- **Common conventions**:
  - **ROS standard**: x forward, y left, z up
  - **Camera standard**: z forward, x right, y down
  - **OpenGL standard**: x right, y up, z backward
